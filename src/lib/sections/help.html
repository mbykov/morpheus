help:

just copy Crtl+C Chinese text everywhere on a desktop

 

ambiguities: sometimes a popup will appear itself, because Morphtus can not find the only variant of the segmentation

recursive segmentation: long (more then one symbol) segment can be segmented further with mouse-click

 

shift disables showing dictionaries (to move mouse freely)

shift + mouse-wheel scrolls other pane (to scroll dicts pane without moving mouse to it)

 

To change dictionaries order, click on it's name at top of a right pane:

 

To get the desired order of displaying dictionaries, click the installed dictionaries in reverse order. For example, if you want to get order DE, FR, EN - choose EN, it will be the first, then click FR, FR will be the first, and EN the second. Then DE, the desired order is obtained.
